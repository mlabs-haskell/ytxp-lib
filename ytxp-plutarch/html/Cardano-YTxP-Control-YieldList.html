newtype YieldListDatum Source #

The YieldListDatum holds a collection of hashes that YieldingScripts can yield to. The length of the datum is checked upon creation in mkYieldListSTMPWrapper to ensure that the length of the list does not exceed the max list length passed as a parameter to that script.
